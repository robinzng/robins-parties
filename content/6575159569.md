Congratulations, you've cracked our pigpen cipher. 

Now let's test your navigational abilities.

Start at the location you just deciphered. 

Travel due west until you hit a new country. 

In the country where you've just arrived, find its largest river. Follow that river to its western-most source. 

The source of the river is also a mountain range. Find that range's tallest peak. 

From that peak, travel to the nearest national capital. 

From the capital, travel due west again until you reach another country. 

The capital of this country lies on a certain sea. Cross this sea to reach another country.
